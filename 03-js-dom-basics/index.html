Why Do Cats Like To Hide?

A big thing to think of is cats have an instinct to AVOID PREDATATION, whether it be from other animals in the household or from other people. A safe spot is a need for everyone, inclduing cats. It's probably the main reason which they go to hide even if it's not on purpose. With a good hiding spot, cats feel more safe and comfortable.
Other reasons a cat is hiding are:

  • illness
  • relaxation
  • overstimulation
  • stress
  • pain
  • imminent birth

A Good Hiding Place

A good hiding place is everything. Cats tend to find some of the strangest places to hide.
Here are a couple of cat quality hiding places:

  • boxes
  • bags (suitcases, purses, backpacks, the works)
  • sinks
  • beds (under the beds and even under the covers)
  • laundry baskets
  • closets
