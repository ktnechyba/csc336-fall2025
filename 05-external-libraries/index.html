About The Cat's Meow

The Cat's Meow is a small, cozy cafe named after the owner's deep love of cats. It has various cat themed foods to satisfy any appetite! All dishes are made in house, and all ingredients are sourced locally. Additionally, we make our own jam and jelly, which you can buy at the farmers market. Come stop by and say hello!

Every month there's a special drink dedicated to our famous cat of the month! Any cat on our cat wall is elligible to become Cat of the Month! We welcome any submissions to our cat wall!
